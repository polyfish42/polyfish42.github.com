Fresh 15

Fresh 15 was a branded recrutiing program. As an unknown tech company, we had a challenge attracting good candidates at tech career fairs. So we decided to stand out by branding our recruiting program, and feature specific aspects of the program to stand out.

We had successfully done this before with our Fantastic 14 campgain.

This year wanted the visual elements to tie more strongly to a message and an emotion. Here we drew on nostalgia for childhood dreams to sell candidates the idea that they could still find that fulfillment at Inflection.

As co-creative director, I was responsible for coming up with the concept, copy, photography, and general ideation. Mariam Briamah was the other creative director, and she did the logo, website design, and branding with me. You should definitely check her website out.
